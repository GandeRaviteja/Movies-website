About TCB

Welcome to TCB (Telugu Cinema Base), your ultimate destination for discovering Telugu cinema. We are passionate movie fans dedicated to providing a clean, fast, and comprehensive platform for exploring the rich world of Tollywood.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make it easy for you to find your next favorite movie. Whether you're looking for a new release, a top-rated classic, or a hidden gem in a specific genre, TCB is designed to help you navigate the vast library of Telugu films with ease.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Database: Explore thousands of movies, from the latest blockbusters to timeless classics, all in one place.
  • Advanced Filtering: Find exactly what you're looking for with our powerful genre, year, and rating filters.
  • Where to Watch: Get up-to-date details on where to stream movies on popular OTT platforms in India.
  • Trailers & Summaries: Watch trailers and read summaries to get a sneak peek before you decide on your next movie night.

This website is a fan-made project and is not affiliated with any production house. All movie data, including posters, summaries, ratings, and trailers, is provided by The Movie Database (TMDb). TCB uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.
